# Content AI - SEO Generator Policy

This policy document explains the expected use, data handling approach, and merchant responsibilities for Content AI - SEO Generator. It is intended as app documentation and should be reviewed with legal counsel before being used as a public legal policy.

## App Purpose

Content AI - SEO Generator helps Shopify merchants generate, review, and manage AI-assisted store content, including product descriptions, collection descriptions, page content, blog content, SEO titles, and SEO descriptions.

## Merchant Responsibility

Merchants are responsible for reviewing all generated content before publishing it. The app provides AI-assisted drafts, but the merchant remains responsible for:

- Product claim accuracy
- Pricing accuracy
- Shipping, refund, warranty, and policy accuracy
- Brand voice and formatting
- Legal and regulatory compliance
- Intellectual property compliance
- Final approval before saving content to Shopify

## AI Content Accuracy

AI-generated content may be incomplete, inaccurate, repetitive, or unsuitable for a specific product or market. Merchants should not treat generated output as verified factual content. Any claims about product performance, materials, safety, certifications, health benefits, guarantees, or compliance standards must be checked before publication.

## Prohibited Use

Merchants should not use the app to create or publish:

- Misleading or deceptive product claims
- Fake customer reviews or testimonials
- False certifications, guarantees, awards, or endorsements
- Content that infringes third-party intellectual property
- Discriminatory, abusive, or unlawful content
- Medical, legal, financial, or safety claims that have not been properly verified
- Content that violates Shopify policies or applicable laws

## Data Access

The app accesses Shopify store data needed to provide its features. Depending on the feature used, this may include product, collection, page, blog, SEO, and shop information. The app uses this data to display store resources, generate content, save merchant-approved updates, track usage, and support billing or credit limits.

## Data Storage

The app may store operational data needed for the service, such as:

- Shopify session information
- Generated content records
- App settings
- Template selections
- Usage and credit information
- Billing plan information
- Review prompt status or merchant feedback submitted in the app

Store data should be retained only as long as needed to operate the app, provide support, meet compliance requirements, and maintain billing or usage history.

## AI Provider Processing

When merchants generate content, relevant prompt and store content may be sent to the configured AI provider for processing. Merchants should avoid entering sensitive personal data, payment data, or private customer information into prompts or custom instructions.

## Customer Data

The app is designed for merchant store content workflows and should not require merchants to enter customer personal data into AI prompts. If customer data is encountered through Shopify privacy webhooks or platform requirements, it should be handled according to Shopify privacy requirements.

## Privacy Webhooks

The app includes Shopify privacy compliance webhook endpoints for customer data requests, customer redact requests, shop redact requests, app uninstall, and scope update events. These webhooks support Shopify platform privacy requirements.

## App Uninstall

When a merchant uninstalls the app, the app should stop accessing the shop and handle uninstall cleanup according to Shopify requirements. Some operational records may be retained temporarily where needed for security, fraud prevention, billing, support, legal compliance, or backup processes.

## Security

The app should use Shopify authentication, session handling, and HMAC verification where required. Access tokens, API keys, and provider credentials must be protected and should not be exposed in client-side code or public documentation.

## Billing and Credits

Some features may depend on the merchant's active billing plan or available credits. Plan names, prices, credit limits, and renewal behavior are shown inside the app. Merchants should review plan details before purchasing or changing plans.

## Support

Support is available at support@m2webdesigning.com. Merchants should include their shop domain, a description of the issue, screenshots where useful, and the page or workflow where the issue occurred.

## Policy Updates

This document may be updated as the app changes. Merchants should review the latest version when app features, data handling, AI providers, billing, or compliance requirements change.
