# Content AI - SEO Generator FAQ

## What does Content AI - SEO Generator do?

Content AI - SEO Generator helps Shopify merchants generate and manage AI-assisted SEO content for products, collections, pages, and blog content. It can create product descriptions, meta titles, meta descriptions, page body content, and other store content based on your selected templates and settings.

## Which Shopify content types are supported?

The app supports content workflows for:

- Products
- Collections
- Collection products
- Pages
- Blog posts
- SEO metadata

## Can I review content before publishing it?

Yes. Generated content is shown inside the app before it is applied to Shopify. Merchants should review generated text for accuracy, brand voice, product claims, pricing, policies, and compliance before saving it to the store.

## Does the app automatically publish AI content?

No. The app is designed around merchant review and action. Content is generated and managed in the app, then applied only when the merchant chooses to update the relevant Shopify resource.

## What are templates?

Templates are reusable prompts that guide the AI output. They help keep content consistent across products, collections, pages, blog posts, and SEO fields.

## Can I change language, tone, and output settings?

Yes. The Settings page lets merchants configure default generation preferences such as language, tone, word count, format, keywords, and AI model preferences where available.

## What is Content Management?

Content Management lets merchants review, edit, and apply generated content across supported resource types. It is intended to help manage generated copy in one workflow instead of updating each item manually.

## What does Analytics show?

Analytics helps merchants review SEO coverage, generated content activity, and usage indicators such as generation history or credit usage, depending on the active plan and available data.

## Why do I see a Shopify login or shop-domain screen?

This usually means the embedded Shopify session context was missing from the URL. Open the app from the Shopify admin or use in-app navigation so the `shop`, `host`, and `embedded` parameters are preserved.

## Does AI-generated content require review?

Yes. AI output can contain inaccurate, incomplete, duplicated, or unsupported claims. Merchants are responsible for reviewing all generated content before publishing it to their Shopify store.

## What should I avoid generating?

Avoid publishing content that includes:

- Unsupported medical, legal, financial, or safety claims
- Fake reviews, fake certifications, or fake guarantees
- Incorrect shipping, refund, warranty, or pricing information
- Misleading product claims
- Content copied from third-party sources without permission

## How are credits used?

Credits are used when generating AI content. Credit limits and renewals depend on the merchant's active plan or purchased credit package.

## Can I change plans?

Yes. Plan and pricing options are available from the app's Pricing page.

## How do I get support?

Merchants can contact support at support@m2webdesigning.com. The dashboard also includes support links for email, WhatsApp, and setup call booking where available.
